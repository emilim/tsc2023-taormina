The registration fee is EURO 550 (EURO 450 for students). Dinner tickets for the social event will be available for late purchase.

23, January 2023 registration opens

A selection of hotels with pre-reserved rooms for accommodation will soon be available. Room rates in Taormina range between 100 and 600 EURO per night and per person
