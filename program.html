Program


TSC 2023 includes plenary sessions, in-depth workshops, concurrent presentations, poster sessions on a glamorous rooftop in the center of Taormina, and book and technology exhibits. The program overview provides an informative guide, showing the surface structure of the conference, with each day on a double page.

Abstracts of workshops, special events, east-west religions forum, plenary talks, concurrent sessions and posters are all included in the abstracts section of the conference booklet. Poster presenters will be able to locate whether their presentation is scheduled for the session on Wednesday June 26 or Thursday June 27.

A/V documentations of all plenary sessions eventually will be available online.
