News

We are sorry to announce that Cristof Koch will not be able to attend the conference as previously announced. We like to share his own words:

I am terribly sorry but I will have to cancel my participation in the Sicily conference. After ten years as the Chief Scientist and then the President of the ‘Allen Institute for Brain Science’ in Seattle, I will be stepping down from my executive position at the end of May, returning to a simpler life as a humble scientist, a development I’m quite happy about. I had not realized that this change of the guard at the Institute goes hand-in-hand with all sorts of meetings with affected personnel, HR, and AllStaff announcements. It is during this time that the Sicily Conference takes place, a conference at which I looked forward to catching up with many friends and colleagues in our community.

I look forward to meeting with you all in 2024 in Tucson.

Christof Koch

April 6th, 2023
