Travel

International travelers are recommended to book their flight into Catania Fontanarossa International Airport offering connections from virtually every country worldwide (aeroporto.catania.it).

Bus schedules from Catania airport to Taormina: checkmybus.it/catania-aeroporto/taormina

Accommodation

There are blocked reservations available at a number of three-star to five-star hotels in walking distance from the Center.

A selection of hotels with pre-reserved rooms for accommodation is available through the registration app where the detailed hotel info is available. The available hotels are the following:

  • Hotel Isabella***
  • Hotel Metropole Maison D'Hotes *****
  • Hotel Monte Tauro ****
  • Hotel Vello D'Oro ***
  • Hotel Villa Diodoro ****
  • Hotel Villa Paradiso ****
  • Porta del Tocco Design Rooms ***
  • Splendid Hotel Taormina****

However, since Taormina is an ideal venue there are plenty of other options available and participants are free to make directly hotel bookings at the hotel of their choice. Room rates in Taormina range between 100 and 600 EURO per night and per person.
